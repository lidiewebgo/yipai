About us

Foshan Yipai Office Furniture is a modern office furniture enterprise specialized in design, production and sales. Since the establishment of our company, we have focused on the research and production of modern mid-to-high-end office furniture, actively promoted the furniture culture, and integrated the international trend, using unlimited creative design to produce a large number of new products with beautiful appearance, comfortable sitting and leading fashion .

The company was founded in 2007 and has been rooting in products, and has steadily moved forward, from the previous plant of more than 1,000 square meters to more than 20,000 square meters. The country's first fixed recliner chair was popular in the market in 2008. In 2010, our company introduced a new functional swivel chair that is removable and easy to install, changing the history that traditional chairs cannot be disassembled. This design enhances the firmness of the chair, reduces the cost of transportation and storage, and is economical, low-carbon, and environmentally friendly. In 2015, we established the Mesh Chair Division, which has an independent mold and uses original German design. In 2018, our research and development team made another major breakthrough, using down and latex as the chair filler for the first time. In September 2018, the product's first appearance on the Shanghai Hongqiao Exhibition overturned the furniture industry. In 2019, this kind of product was listed on a domestic TV hit series, once again making this product popular in the whole market. In 2020, our team has designed a super leisure chair.

Integrity, responsibility, innovation, and quality have always been our ever-changing ideas. Customer satisfaction is our great glory, but also our pursuit and goal. Our company is willing to join hands with friends to create a better tomorrow.
